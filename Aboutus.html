About us

Azesavant Software Solutions Pvt Ltd.We are transforming from ClickNTouch Technologies(2018 - 2023) to Azesavant Software Solutions Pvt Ltd. Azesavant Software Solutions, nestled in the vibrant tech hub of Chennai, India, has emerged as a prominent player in the domain of educational technology and cloud web development. With a rich history spanning five years, the company has dedicated itself to serving colleges and universities, striving to transform the educational sector through innovative digital solutions.

At its inception, Azesavant set out with a vision to bridge the gap between educational institutions and the rapidly evolving digital landscape. Recognizing the unique challenges and opportunities in higher education, the company has tailored its services to cater specifically to this sector. Whether it's developing state-of-the-art online learning platforms, managing intricate student information systems, or deploying cloud-based solutions for administrative efficiency, Azesavant has consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible in educational technology.
