Logistics

The comprehensive management of resources, transportation, and storage within a supply chain, ensuring that goods and information move efficiently from origin to destination. In business, logistics encompasses activities like inventory management, warehousing, order fulfillment, and the coordination of transportation modes, such as trucking, shipping, and air freight.

The primary goal of logistics is to optimize the flow of goods and services, minimize costs, and meet customer demands with accuracy and speed. Modern logistics often relies on technology for real-time tracking, data analytics, and automation to streamline operations, reduce errors, and enhance customer satisfaction. It is a critical component across industries, as efficient logistics helps businesses respond to market changes, manage supply chain disruptions, and gain a competitive edge by delivering products and services promptly and reliably.

Logistics plays a pivotal role in connecting production with consumption, ensuring that goods, materials, and services flow smoothly from manufacturers to end consumers.
